Sample
""""""

Do not forget to add DSS.
0.5 ul of 100 mM DSS stock solution is sufficient for a 250 ul sample.

Example:

#. 237  ul protein sample in buffer
#. 12.5 ul D2O
#. 0.5  ul DSS stock solution


Calibration
